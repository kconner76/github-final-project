# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, race, or sexual orientation.

## Our Standards
*   **Positive Behavior:** Empathy, kindness, respect for diverse viewpoints, constructive feedback, and community focus.
*   **Unacceptable Behavior:** Harassment, sexualized language, trolling, or posting private information.

## Enforcement
*   **Responsibilities:** Community leaders will enforce these standards.
*   **Scope:** Applies to all project spaces and public representation.
*   **Reporting:** Contact community leaders at **[INSERT EMAIL ADDRESS]**.
*   **Guidelines:** Corrective actions range from warnings to permanent bans.
